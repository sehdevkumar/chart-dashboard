Bar Chart

Suitable for comparing categorical data. Used to display and compare data across different categories or groups. Can be either vertical (column chart) or horizontal (bar chart).

Pie Chart

Best for displaying parts of a whole. Useful for representing proportions or percentages. Avoid using too many segments for clarity.
